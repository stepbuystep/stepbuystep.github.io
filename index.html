Biography

I am currently a second-year PhD student at Nanyang Technological University (SCSE), supervised by Prof. Weichen Liu. I graduated from Shanghai Jiao Tong University with a B.Eng. from School of Electronic Information and Electrical Engineering (2015 Sep to 2019 June). My main research interest lies in the intersection of hardware and artificial intelligence, which aims to build hardware-friendly AI solutions in terms of latency and power. Life is about doing tradeoffs, so you can't expect to grasp efficiency & accuracy and win them all. : )
